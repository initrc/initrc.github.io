Autumn in Leavenworth

2013.10.22

Summer is gone. I’m not a rain hater, but I do feel I’m short of sunshine. Even when the weather forecast says sunny, it only lasts a couple of hours. After summer, the mountains block the vapor from the west and make it miserably humid, but on the other hand, it keeps the east side of the mountains dry and wonderful!

It was sunny in Leavenworth at 67F. After having some non-genuine German cuisine (sausage and cream potato soup), I started probably my last hike of the year from Enchantment Park.
