Travel

Rainy Mount Rainier

2015.8.31

Spent the weekend at Mount Rainier with the team. Somehow I feel the experience was like P4G. Definitely had a great time and did quite a few things that I wouldn’t do alone. Driving, shopping, cooking, eating, playing cards, music and games in the car, and lots of photos. Best team ever :)

The weather was terrible. It was raining heavily on Saturday when we were hiking at Paradise. I still can’t believe we completed the Skyline trail.

Sunday was better, not raining, but cloudy and cold. We hiked on two trails again at Sunrise. Although the weather really challenged my photo editing, it’s the first time my photo was in the Popular feed on 500px!
